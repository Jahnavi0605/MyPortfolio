about_me

The analyst behind
the data.

I have been working in data for over five years now, and the thing I love most about this job is the moment a client looks at a dashboard and finally sees something they could not see before. That is what I build towards every time.

At Sloka Technologies I work directly with external clients on their operations data. I build their Power BI and Tableau dashboards, write the SQL and Python pipelines that feed them, and apply statistical analysis and process mining to find where their workflows are quietly losing time and money.

What makes me a bit unusual is the breadth. Not many analysts combine BI development, R statistical modelling, Celonis process mining and Six Sigma in the same role. I picked all of these up because each one fills a gap the others cannot. Together they mean I can go from raw messy data all the way to a board-ready recommendation without handing off to anyone else.
